ABOUT US

OUR STORY

The Green Bean Roasting Co. was born from a passion for exceptional coffee and a deep-rooted commitment to our local community. What began as a small dream in a Pennsylvania kitchen has grown into a thriving coffee roastery dedicated to freshness, transparency, and flavor.

Our founder, a proud graduate of York College of Pennsylvania, combined a love of science, craftsmanship, and community to create something special—coffee that tells a story from seed to cup. Every bean we roast is thoughtfully selected from responsible growers around the world, then roasted in small batches right here at home to highlight the unique flavor profiles of each origin.

We believe that great coffee isn’t just about the brew—it’s about the experience. Whether you’re grabbing a quick cup before work or enjoying a slow morning at home, we want each sip to feel personal, intentional, and delicious.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

At The Green Bean Roasting Co., we believe coffee is more than just a beverage—it’s a ritual, a connector, and a celebration of people and place.

We’re committed to sourcing beans in a way that uplifts farmers and protects the environment. Our direct relationships with growers ensure fair wages, sustainable practices, and quality you can taste. We value transparency and aim to share the journey of every bean—from farm to roast to your cup.

More than anything, we believe in community. Whether you're a longtime customer or someone trying our coffee for the first time, you’re part of our extended family. We’re here to fuel your mornings, spark your conversations, and help create those moments that matter.

Because at Green Bean, it’s not just about the roast—it’s about the connection.
