ABOUT US

OUR STORY

We started Green Bean Coffee with a simple goal: deliver freshly roasted coffee to our community. Every bean is sourced with care and roasted to highlight its unique flavors.

Our founder studied at York College of Pennsylvania and brings that dedication to quality into every roast.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Great coffee brings people together. We believe in sustainable sourcing and building long lasting relationships with our farmers and customers.
